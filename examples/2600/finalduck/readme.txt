===========================
FINAL DUCK
for Atari 2600

---------------------------

by Andrew G. Crowell
===========================

Eat the bread!
Two ducks face against each other.
First player to 10 points wins.
If you shoot the other player, they will be knocked back, and sometimes lose a point.

Controls
--------

Uses the 2600 joystick.

Directionals = move
Button = fire missile / eat

How to Open
-----------

The ROM is for an Atari 2600. For the best experience, download the ROM onto a
flash cartridge such as the Harmony Cartridge, and play on your Atari 2600 VCS console.
Altenatively, use the Stella emulator.
